The phrase "Dhoni is mass" refers to MS Dhoni's immense popularity and "swag," which are celebrated by fans who use terms like "mass," "Thala," and "whistle podu" to show their adoration. The term "mass" is often used in fan culture to describe a player's powerful, stylish, and impactful presence, particularly when they make a grand entry onto a field or execute a brilliant shot.
